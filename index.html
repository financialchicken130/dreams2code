Our Mission

Dreams2Code is a non-profit organization with the vision of transforming our passion for computer science into education for underserved communities. Together, we aspire to spread this passion for technology through hosting hackathons, free computing classes, and aiding local businesses with digital needs. Dreams2Code strives to empower communities, fostering a more inclusive and technologically vibrant future.

Forging a new generation of aspiring new coders

Why Computer Science?

Project-Building

Through our program, students will not only learn how to use and manipulate various computer science and coding ideas, but will eventually apply such concepts in the forrm of projects. Not only do these prorjects demonstrate the student's growth, but also demonstrates management skills needed to maintain a larger project.

Invaluable Skills

In today's world, computer science, and the skills that pursuing it's education provides, have become invaluable and priceless. Not only do countless jobs and opportunities exist for students and adults alike in the world of computer science, but for many, it is a hobby. Through this program, we encourage students to think in this same mindset.

Fostering Creativity

Due to the vastness of both computer science theory, and coding itself, platforms and languages such as Java, Python and C++ offer boundless creativity, allowing students to create whatever their heart desire. These classes will teach students how to harness their creative energy to prorduce programs that they will be proud of, and have fun along the way.

Our Services

Computing Classes

We teach children ages 10-14 computer science at local libraries. Students start from the basics and slowly learn how to create amazing projects using advanced programming concepts. At it's core, this consists of a 10-week training course, but can be expanded if time permits. Sign up today!

Helping Local Businesses

We help local businesses in the Central Jersey area with their digital needs. We create websites, mobile apps, and help with digital marketing. This is all preformed through our developer department, and led by our Director of Technology.

Hackathons and Other Events

We plan to host local hackathons and other events to bring the community together. This would include partnerships with local schools, and inviting possible sponsors. Please stay tuned for details.
